FAQ

Referral Questions

Quick answers about referring friends and earning rewards.

Rewards are applied instantly once your friend completes their first paid appointment. There is no waiting period — as soon as they check out, both your account and theirs are credited.

No limit. Refer as many friends as you like and keep earning. The more you share, the more rewards you unlock — from salon credit all the way up to luxury beauty packages.

Log into your dashboard anytime to see how many friends have signed up, which rewards you have earned, and how close you are to the next milestone. Everything updates in real time.

The referral program is for new clients only. Your friend must be booking their first appointment with us for the referral to count and for rewards to be issued.

Referral credits are valid for 12 months from the date they are issued. You will receive a reminder 30 days before expiry so you never miss a chance to use them.
